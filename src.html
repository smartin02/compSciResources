Study Tips

Anyone can learn Physics! Here is how to study for it!

Blooms Taxonomy can be applied to studying for college level Physics. By starting with the knowledge of fundamental concepts and the application/analysis of Physics principles, anyone can truly understand any concept in Physics. Instead, when you work out a practice problem we have given you, make sure that you can explain why and when you would make each step in your solution. Try to refer back to the five W's (who, what, when, where, why). Try to justify each step with more than a ‘just because’ statement, that way you avoid tricking yourself into believing you understand a topic.

Health Matters!

Look before you leap and be sure to manage your own health before jumping into your studies. This includes:

Avoiding sleep deprivation, we learn best when alert!

Eating nutritious food, food is fuel for the brain!

Stay hydrated! Coffee and Red Bull is not water.

Exercise! Studies show that physical activity can help you clear brain fog, think, learn, and problem-solve (CDC).
